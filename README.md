# 🏀 R-Ball — The Card Game

A browser-based basketball card game. Play solo against AI or challenge a friend in real-time multiplayer.

🎮 **[Play now](https://zomani.github.io/r-ball/)**

---

## How to play

Each turn, you play a card from your deck. First to **21 points** wins.

- **You have the ball** → play a Dunk or Shot card to score
- **Opponent has the ball** → play a Defense card to steal it
- **Nothing works?** → skip your turn and wait

When someone shoots, the opponent's best available Defense card automatically triggers to attempt a block. If it's on cooldown — the shot goes in.

---

## Card types

| Type | What it does |
|------|-------------|
| 🏀 Dunk | Score 2 pts |
| 🎯 Shot | Score 2 or 3 pts |
| ⚡ Superpower | Score 3 pts — very hard to pull off |
| 🛡️ Defense | Steal the ball or block a shot |
| 👁️ Ability | Crossover, fake, pass — gain an edge |

Every card has a **cooldown** — after playing it, you wait a few turns before using it again. Defense card cooldowns depend on your player's DEF stat.

---

## Multiplayer

Click **Create Room**, share the code with a friend, they paste it and join. Uses WebRTC — no server needed, just a browser.

---

## Progression

Win matches to earn credits. Spend them in the Shop to unlock new cards via boosters.
